Marketing Research Project

Identifying Factors Affecting Consumer Purchase Intention of ABC Herbal (AYUR) Tea

Institution: Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM)

Date of Completion: March 2024

Project Type: Quantitative Research

Keywords: Trust, Price, Quality, Brand Awareness, Purchase Intention

Abstract

This research investigates the decline in market share of AYUR Tea by analyzing key factors such as trust, price, quality, and brand awareness affecting consumer purchase intention in the Western Province.

Key Highlights

  • Trust accounts for 56.3% variance in purchase intention.
  • Price influences purchase intention by 36.4%.
  • Brand awareness and perceived quality show positive correlations with purchase intention.
